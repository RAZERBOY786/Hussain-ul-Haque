About Hussain Ul Haque

  • Date of Birth: November 2nd, 1949 (10th Moharram)
  • Place of Birth: Sasaram, Bihar, India
  • Date of Passing: December 23, 2021 (18th Jamadi-awwal), Patna, Bihar
  • Father’s Name: Maulana Anwarul Haque Shahoodi
  • Spouse: Mrs. Nishat Israr
  • Children: Two Daughters and Two Sons

Career milestones of Hussain Ul Haque from 1972 to 2014.

Career Timeline

  • 1972 Lecturer, Guru Govind Singh College, Patna
  • 1974 Lecturer, Santal Pargana College, Dumka, Jharkhand
  • 1976 Lecturer, Gaya College, Gaya
  • 1980 Transferred to P.G. Department of Urdu, Magadh University
  • 1986 Promoted to Reader, Magadh University
  • 1990 Head of Department of Persian, Magadh University
  • 2012 Appointed Proctor, Magadh University
  • 2014 Retired as Head of P.G. Department, Magadh University
