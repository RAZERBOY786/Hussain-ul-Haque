Current Work on Hussain ul Haque

Ongoing research and publication efforts related to Hussain Ul Haque’s literary contributions.

  • 15 research scholars are pursuing PhD studies on Hussain Ul Haque’s work at various universities in India.
  • A collection of papers presented at the 2024 webinar on Hussain Ul Haque’s work is being prepared for publication.
  • Reprint of Hussain ul Haque’s first novel, Bolo Mat Chup Raho, is in progress.
